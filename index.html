MedTracker is your personal home medicine inventory management system. Track expiry dates, manage dosage schedules, and never run out of essential medicines.

Designed following Shneiderman's 8 Golden Rules of Interface Design to ensure a consistent, efficient, and user-friendly experience.

DEMO Version · HCI Group 8 Project

🏆 Shneiderman's 8 Golden Rules ▼
1
Strive for Consistency — Uniform button styles, colors, and typography across all pages.
2
Seek Universal Usability — Color-coded status, readable fonts, and clear labels for all skill levels.
3
Offer Informative Feedback — Toast notifications confirm every action. Status badges give visual feedback.
4
Design Dialogs to Yield Closure — Modals have clear Cancel and Save with success confirmation.
5
Prevent Errors — Form validation highlights empty required fields. Confirm before delete.
6
Permit Easy Reversal — Undo button in Activity Log restores deleted items.
7
Keep Users in Control — Sidebar navigation always accessible. Sort, filter, and search give full control.
8
Reduce Memory Load — Status shown on each card. Auto-suggestions in forms reduce typing effort.
